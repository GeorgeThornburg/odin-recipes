Today we're going to be showing you how to make cereal!

So keep your Pajamas on! We don't have to leave the house!

You'll need:

  • milk
  • your choice of cereal
  • bowl
  • spoon

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Ok start off by putting the bowl on your head (make sure the open end is towards the sky)
  2. Pour cereal into bowl
  3. Pour milk in after that (stop when milk starts pouring down your face)
  4. enjoy!
